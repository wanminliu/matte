#### 2323 Förenkla uttrycken.

a). $3\left( x - \frac{y}{3} \right)\left( 2y + \frac{1}{3}\right)$

b). $\frac{\left(\frac{x}{2}-y\right)\left(y-\frac{x}{2}\right)}{2}$

---

**Lösning.**

a).

$$
\begin{aligned}
3\left( x - \frac{y}{3} \right)\left( 2y + \frac{1}{3} \right)
&= (3x - y)\left( 2y + \frac{1}{3} \right) \\
&= 3x \cdot 2y + 3x \cdot \frac{1}{3} - y \cdot 2y - y \cdot \frac{1}{3} \\
&= 6xy + x - 2y^2 - \frac{1}{3}y
\end{aligned}
$$

b).

$$
\begin{aligned}
\frac{\left(\frac{x}{2}-y\right)\left(y-\frac{x}{2}\right)}{2}
&= \frac{\frac{x}{2}\cdot y - \frac{x}{2}\cdot \frac{x}{2} - y\cdot y + y\cdot \frac{x}{2}}{2} \\
&= \frac{\frac{xy}{2} - \frac{x^2}{4} - y^2 + \frac{xy}{2}}{2} \\
&= \frac{xy - \frac{x^2}{4} - y^2}{2} \\
&= \frac{xy}{2} - \frac{x^2}{8} - \frac{y^2}{2}
\end{aligned}
$$

#### Förenkla (Upp 22 i sidan 152)

a). $\frac{x^{-10}+x^{-11}}{x^{-10}}$

b). $\left(\frac{xy^2+xy^2}{x^2 y}\right)^{-1}$

---

**Lösning.**

a).

$$
\begin{aligned}
\frac{x^{-10}+x^{-11}}{x^{-10}}
&= \frac{(x^{-10}+x^{-11})\cdot x^{10}}{x^{-10}\cdot x^{10}} \\
&= \frac{x^{-10+10}+x^{-11+10}}{x^{-10+10}} \\
&= \frac{1 + x^{-1}}{1} \\
&= 1 + x^{-1}
\end{aligned}
$$

**Alternativ:**

$$
\begin{aligned}
1 + x^{-1} = 1 + \frac{1}{x}
\end{aligned}
$$

**Alternativ:**

$$
\begin{aligned}
\frac{x^{-10}+x^{-11}}{x^{-10}}
&= \frac{(x^{-10}+x^{-11})\cdot x^{11}}{x^{-10}\cdot x^{11}} \\
&= \frac{x + 1}{x}
\end{aligned}
$$

**Förklaring:**

$$
\begin{aligned}
\frac{x+1}{x} &= \frac{x}{x} + \frac{1}{x} \\
&= 1 + \frac{1}{x}
\end{aligned}
$$

---

b).

$$
\begin{aligned}
\left(\frac{xy^2+xy^2}{x^2 y}\right)^{-1}
&= \left(\frac{2xy^2}{x^2 y}\right)^{-1} \\
&= \left(\frac{2y}{x}\right)^{-1} \\
&= \frac{x}{2y}
\end{aligned}
$$

## Lös ekvationen för variabeln $x$

Faktorisera ekvationen. $a$ är en konstant.

$$
ax - 5x = 6
$$

**Lösning.**

Om $a = 5$ finns det ingen lösning.

Om $a \neq 5$:

$$
\begin{aligned}
ax - 5x &= (a - 5)x \\
(a - 5)x &= 6 \\
x &= \frac{6}{a - 5}
\end{aligned}
$$
