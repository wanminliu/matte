#### 2323 Förenkla uttrycken.

a).  $3\Big( x - \frac{y}{3} \Big) \Big( 2y + \frac{1}{3}\Big)$.

b). $\frac{(x/2-y)(y-x/2)}{2}$.

**Lösning.**
a).  
$$
\begin{align}
3\Big( x - \frac{y}{3} \Big) \Big( 2y + \frac{1}{3}\Big) &= ( 3x - y ) \Big( 2y + \frac{1}{3}\Big) \\
 & = 3x\cdot 2y+3x\cdot \frac{1}{3}-y\cdot 2y-y\frac{1}{3}\\
 & = 6xy+x-2y^2-\frac{1}{3}y.
\end{align}
$$

b).
$$
\begin{align}
\frac{(x/2-y)(y-x/2)}{2} &= \frac{(\frac{x}{2}-y)(y-\frac{x}{2})}{2} \\
 & = \frac{\frac{x}{2}\cdot y-\frac{x}{2}\cdot \frac{x}{2}-y\cdot y+y\cdot \frac{x}{2}}{2}\\
 & = \frac{\frac{xy}{2}-\frac{x^2}{4}-y^2+\frac{xy}{2}}{2}\\
 & = \frac{xy-\frac{x^2}{4}-y^2}{2}\\
 & = \frac{xy}{2}-\frac{x^2}{8}-\frac{y^2}{2}.
\end{align}
$$

#### Förenkla (Upp 22 i sidan 152)

a). $\frac{x^{-10}+x^{-11}}{x^{-10}}$
b). $\Big(\frac{xy^2+xy^2}{x^2 y}\Big)^{-1}$

**Lösning.**
a).  
$$
\begin{align}
\frac{x^{-10}+x^{-11}}{x^{-10}} &= \frac{(x^{-10}+x^{-11})}{x^{-10}}\\
&= \frac{(x^{-10}+x^{-11})\cdot x^{10}}{x^{-10}\cdot x^{10}} \\
 & = \frac{x^{-10}\cdot x^{10}+x^{-11}\cdot x^{10}}{x^{-10}\cdot x^{10}} \\
 & = \frac{x^{-10+10}+x^{-11+10}}{x^{-10+10}}\\
 & = \frac{1+x^{-1}}{1} \\
 & = 1+x^{-1}.
\end{align}
$$

Alternativ.

$$
\begin{align}
\frac{x^{-10}+x^{-11}}{x^{-10}} &= \frac{(x^{-10}+x^{-11})}{x^{-10}}\\
 & \cdots \\
 & = 1+x^{-1}\\
 & = 1+\frac{1}{x}.
\end{align}
$$

Alternativ.

$$
\begin{align}
\frac{x^{-10}+x^{-11}}{x^{-10}} &= \frac{(x^{-10}+x^{-11})\cdot x^{11}}{x^{-10}\cdot x^{11}}\\
 & \cdots \\
 & = \frac{x+1}{x}.
\end{align}
$$

Förklara:

$$
\begin{align}
 \frac{x+1}{x} & = \frac{x}{x}+\frac{1}{x}\\
  & = 1+\frac{1}{x}.
\end{align}
$$

b).  
$$
\begin{align}
\Big(\frac{xy^2+xy^2}{x^2 y}\Big)^{-1} &= \Big(\frac{2xy^2}{x^2 y}\Big)^{-1}\\
&= \Big(\frac{2\cdot xy\cdot y}{xy\cdot x}\Big)^{-1} \\
 & = \Big(\frac{2\cdot y}{ x}\Big)^{-1} \\
 & = \frac{x}{2y}.
\end{align}
$$
